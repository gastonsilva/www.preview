NAME
       rm - remove files or directories

SYNOPSIS
       {keyword}rm{/keyword} [OPTION]... FILE...

DESCRIPTION
       {keyword}rm{/keyword} removes each specified file. By default, it does not remove
       directories.

OPTIONS
       -r, -R, --recursive
              remove directories and their contents recursively. Without this option,
              non-empty directories cannot be removed.
